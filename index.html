The product

Built for beam, made for grip

Every beam routine starts with dry, chalked feet. The ChalkBox gives gymnasts a compact, sturdy spot to chalk up before mounting — right at the base of the beam, right where they need it.

✋

Better foot grip

A wide, low-lip design lets gymnasts fully coat the soles and sides of their feet in chalk before every beam pass — no more slipping off the beam.

🎨

Personalized, not generic

Your gym name and gymnast's name are printed on the lid in one color of your choice, and the box itself comes in a color that matches your team.

🎒

Durable & portable

Lightweight, stackable, and built to survive being tossed in a gym bag between meets — with a snap-lid to keep chalk contained in transit.

About

Meet the founder

The gymnast behind the box.

Ashleigh Deckert

Level 9 Gymnast High School Student Future Engineer

Ashleigh is a Level 9 gymnast and high schooler who spends as much time on the beam as she does behind a keyboard. Chalk boxes never seemed to fit gymnasts, so she set out to design one that does — combining her firsthand experience in the gym with a growing passion for engineering and computer science. ChalkBox Co. is the result: a box built by a gymnast, for gymnasts.
